MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology

Located in Bengaluru, Ramaiah Institute of Technology (RIT) is affiliated to the Visvesvaraya Technological University (VTU), which is headquartered in Belgaum. Set up in the year 1962, this autonomous private engineering college has been offering postgraduate and undergraduate programmes since August 2007. Although, VTU awards degrees, the college is free to frame its curricula and schemes of study. The college offers various undergraduate programmes in diverse streams like civil engineering, mechanical engineering, chemical engineering, biotechnology, and medical electronics. It also offers MCA and MTech in different streams, in addition to providing research programmes in various departments that lead to PhD/MSc (Engineering).

Ramaiah Institute of Technology Admission Process 2023

Students seeking admission in Bachelor of Engineering and Bachelor of Architecture programmes at RIT need to clear KEA/COMEDK (Consortium of Medical, Engineering and Dental Colleges of Karnataka) entrance exam. However, the eligibility criteria for enrolment in BE program at RIT is securing at least 45% in 10+2 examinations with mathematics and physics as compulsory subjects. In addition, students should have passed 10+2 exams with 50% in mathematics, physics, and chemistry, apart from securing 50% in aggregate at the 10+2 level. For admission to the MBA programme, students need to appear for KMAT/CMAT/MAT/PGCET, while for MCA, students should have cleared KMAT or PGCET. For MTech, students need to have cleared GATE or should have appeared for PGCET of Karnataka. The eligibility criteria for admission to MBA and MCA programmes are 3-year bachelor’s degree from a recognized institute and a minimum of 50% aggregate marks.

Are you looking for admission to MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology but worried about the tough competition for seats? Don’t worry, you can still secure a seat through management quota admission. Here’s everything you need to know about management quota admission in MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology.

Eligibility Criteria:

To be eligible for management quota admission in MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology, you must meet the following criteria

  • You must have passed 10+2 or an equivalent examination from a recognized board with a minimum aggregate of 45% in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • You must have a valid score in any of the following entrance exams: COMEDK, JEE Main, KCET, or any other equivalent exam.
  • You must meet the age limit criteria set by the college.

    • Admission Process

    The admission process for management quota admission in MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology is as follows:

    • You need to fill in the application form for management quota admission available on the college website.
    • You need to submit the required documents along with the application form, including your 10+2 mark sheet, entrance exam scorecard, age proof, and identity proof.
    • Once the college receives your application, they will review your academic performance and other criteria set by the college.

      • If you meet the eligibility criteria, you will be called for a personal interview with the college authorities.

      Based on your academic performance and the personal interview, the college authorities will select the candidates for management quota admission.

    Benefits of Management Quota Admission

    Here are some of the benefits of securing admission through management quota:

    • You can secure a seat in MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology without going through the strenuous entrance examinations.
    • You have a better chance of getting admission as the number of seats under management quota is limited.
    • You can save time and effort in preparing for the entrance exams and instead focus on your academic performance.

    However, it’s important to note that the fees for management quota admission are usually higher than the regular fees. Additionally, management quota admission is available only for a limited number of seats, and competition for these seats can be tough.

    Conclusion

    In conclusion, management quota admission can be a good option for those who want to secure a seat in MS Ramaiah Institute Of Technology without going through the entrance exams. Make sure you meet the eligibility criteria and submit your application on time to increase your chances of getting selected for management quota admission. Good luck!
