Dayananda Sagar College Of Engineering

Established in 1979, Dayananda Sagar College of Engineering (DSCE) is a private and autonomous college, located in Bangalore. It is affiliated to Visvesvaraya Technological University and is run by the Mahatma Gandhi Vidya Peetha Trust (MGVP). DSCE is approved by the University Grants Commission (UGC) and the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE). It has also been accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and the NAAC with Grade ‘A’. Recently, DSCE has also received recognition from the Department of Scientific and Industrial Research (DSIR).

The college has been ranked 25, 43 and 73 for Architecture, Engineering and Private Engineering, respectively by India Today, 2023. At present, DSCE has 21 departments that collectively offer 15 UG and 13 PG courses in multiple specialisations. The college also has multiple professional societies and student clubs in all these departments that strengthen the industry-college interactions. The list of DSCE departments is tabulated below:

DSCE Admission Process

The college offers a facility of an online application process for all programmes. Candidates, who meet the given eligibility criteria, need to register and fill an application form to apply for a particular programme. The course-wise details regarding DSCE admissions are mentioned below:

  • BE admission is based on JEE Main/ COMEDK UGET/ KCET score. For lateral entry admissions, CET scores are considered
  • Admission to MTech programme is based on GATE/ Karnataka PGCET score
  • Candidates willing to seek MBA/ MCA admission must have a valid score in Karnataka PGCET/ MAT/ CAT/ KMAT
  • For admission to the PhD/ MSc (research) programme, VTU conducts ULRAT entrance test

DSCE Placements

DSCE has a placement cell that coordinates with recruiting companies and handles the entire end-to-end placement process. It is provided with a well-equipped auditorium for organising pre-placement talks and well-furnished rooms for training, interviews and counselling. The cell works closely with the final year students and companies hiring fresher students. It is also committed to enhance the employability skills of the students by organising training programmes, including aptitude training programme, soft-skills training, subject/domain-specific training and entrepreneurship development programmes.

In addition to the placement cell, DSCE established a ‘Centre for Innovation & Leadership’ (CIL) in 2006 that bridges the gap between academics and corporate realities. It imparts innovative soft skill training programmes to enable individuals to get placed in top companies. The college bagged the highest salary package of Rs 20 LPA during DSCE placements 2022.

RVCE Bangalore Admission Process

The application process for admissions to BTech commences with the online registrations for JEE Mains/KCET (for Karnataka candidates)/COMEDK (for outside Karnataka candidates), and the application process for admissions to PG courses commences with the online registrations for GATE/PGCET. The application fee can be paid via Bank Challan or Demand Draft (drawn in favor of Executive Director Karnataka Examinations Authority, payable at Bangalore).

Admissions to all the programme is based on the scores obtained in the required entrance exam, followed by the Counseling. Interested candidates can refer to below mentioned details about RVCE admission process:

  • Admission to BTech programme is based on the scores obtained in KCET 2023/ COMEDK 2023/ JEE Mains 2023
  • For the Karnataka residing candidates, the selection to BE course is done on the basis of KCET 2023 (Karnataka Common Entrance Test) conducted by Karnataka Examination Authority (KEA)
  • For the non-Karnataka residing candidates, the selection of the candidates to BE course is done on the basis of marks scored in COMEDK 2023 examination
  • 45% seats are filled through KCET, 30% seats are filled based on COMEDK 2023 and 25% seats are reserved for Management, all merit-based quota
  • 80% seats are reserved for students appearing in GATE 2023 and PGCET 2023 exam and 20% seats are reserved for Management Quota
  • 20% of seats of the MCA programme are given to those applying in Lateral Entry through KEA counseling

Eligibility Criteria:

To qualify for management quota admission at RV College of Engineering, you must satisfy the following requirements:

  • Completion of 10+2 or equivalent examination from a recognized board with at least 45% aggregate in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics.
  • A valid score in any of the following entrance exams: COMEDK, JEE Main, KCET, or equivalent exam.
  • Age limit criteria as specified by the college.
